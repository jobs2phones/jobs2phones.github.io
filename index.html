Jobs2Phones
Helping non-smartphone users find employment

How it works

Gain access to up to date jop posts

Users can sign up to receive job postings from a list of hard-coded search queries, such as 'warehouse philadelphia', 'dishwasher philadelphia', etc. Each hour, jobs2phones updates its job database, containing job postings from the most popular websites on the web.

Only get relevant posts

If a new job posting is found, the Jobs2Phones software looks for certain keywords to evaluate that posting's relevance. A post is currently considered relevant if the employer accepts phone or in-person inquiries.

Get only the important information in a text message

Jobs2Phones then looks for a phone number, address, and wage within the post. This information, along with the post title, is sent in a single SMS text message to each user.

Sign Up

1. Text "start" to "jobs2phones@gmail.com".

You will then receive the following message:

"Welcome to J2P. Reply with the numbers from the list below to get relevant postings. 1.dishwasher 2.warehouse 3.cook.""

2. Text the relevant postings from the list. Example text: "1 3"

You will then receive the following message:

['1','3'] Text "STOP" to end service. Text different numbers to change. Text "LIST" to see the list.

3. To change your preferences, text the new numbers. Example text "1 2 3"

To change your preferences, simply text the new numbers that are relevant. For example, if you change your mind and want to also receive warehouse positions, text "1 2 3" at any time.

Text "STOP" to end the service.

A Typical Story

Joe, a 45 year old Philadelphia resident was recently laid off from a warehouse position that he held for over a decade. He has received a traditional cell phone through the Lifeline Cell Phone program. He has trouble conducting internet searches by himself, so he goes to a local community organization to help him find jobs and fill out applications. However, this community organization is very busy with its clients, so Joe is only able to request appointments once every 2 weeks. The general labor jobs that Joe is looking for are typically filled within days of being posted, meaning he must hope that a posting occurs shortly before one of his job search meetings. Joe signs up for Jobs2Phones. Jobs2Phones is a tool that searches the internet for new job postings hourly. Jobs2Phones finds posts that can be responded to by phone or in-person, and then sends the relevant information in a single SMS. Joe receives a text one day ‘215-222-2222; $10.50/hr; 1234 Lincoln hwy; Part-time warehouse help’, calls, and is able to secure a part-time job at the warehouse.
